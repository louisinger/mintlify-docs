# Welcome to Arkade

Arkade is a revolutionary platform that turns financial contracts into Bitcoin transaction programs. Whether you're building decentralized applications, implementing payment solutions, or developing complex financial instruments, Arkade provides the tools and infrastructure you need.

### Self Custodial
Maintain complete control over your funds with our self-custodial architecture. All funds are secured by presigned exits enforced onchain, ensuring you never lose control of your assets.

### Unified Liquidity
Access thousands of off-chain operations aggregated into a single, secure onchain anchor. Our unified liquidity pool ensures maximum scalability and efficiency for all operations.
