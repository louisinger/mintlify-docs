description: "Explains VTXO ownership and self-custody in Arkade."

[VTXO ownership](/learn/pillars/vtxos#vtxo-structure) means being in control of a presigned Bitcoin transaction.

If the operator becomes unresponsive or malicious, users can simply broadcast their presigned transaction on Bitcoin.
